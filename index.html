ABOUT US

Potemall safaris is a well-established tours and travel company, tested and proven to handle local (within East Africa) safaris and events with our very able team of experts who enjoy and love what they do ,thus ensuring our beloved clients get maximum satisfaction and value for their money.
Our specialty is the ability to design unique tours and safaris to fit every type of tourist, from the budget to the luxury providing a unique opportunity to combine bush and beach adventures with East Africa’s spectacular scenery offering a chance to explore the world’s hidden treasures!
If you’re looking for a vacation that provides a huge sense of personal recreation while still being fun, exciting, relaxing, adventurous and rewarding, we suggest you check out our wide range of tours and travel packages.

OUR VISION

To be the number 1 safari and tour company in East Africa in the tourism industry by offering outstanding customer service, breathtaking and memorable experiences at an unbelievable prices that will leave our highly esteemed customers feeling rejuvenated and opportunity to recreate and reinvent themselves adopting a positive and new outlook or view of the world.
